## Problem

The user requested breaking down the implementation steps into very small parts (e.g., 20 to 100 micro tasks). While the ROADMAP.md has 20 initial sub-steps, we need an even more granular micro-task breakdown before starting heavy coding, or at least a structure to generate them dynamically during execution.

## Solution

Create a detailed breakdown checklist or utilize the GSD phase planning (`gsd-plan-phase`) to decompose the first phase into highly granular, executable micro-tasks (e.g., 1 task = 1 file or 1 function).
